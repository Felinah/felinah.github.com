Journal of a glitchy nose

On the 7th of March 2020 I have lost my sense of smell.
It might have been corona, I don't know, but for almost 3 months my nose has been blinded.

Physiotherapy for the nose

Recently I have discovered that if you loose your sense of smell (if your nervous symptom is damaged), it can take a really long time to get it back. I have therefore started a programme of smell training. Hereby I will document how this evolves in the next weeks.

The first set I am using contains the following smells:

  • Roses
  • Lemon
  • Cloves
  • Eucalyptus
