Journal of a glitchy nose

On the 7th of March 2020 I have lost my sense of smell.
It might have been corona, I don't know, but for almost 3 months my nose has been blinded.

Poem for noses

Smelling used to be my favorite sense
It's also because I have a big nose I guess 👃
What can I do with this giant on my face
If I can't sense? 😔

Three months in the dark 🌑
I have endured
No stimulation
No sensation 🙊

First thing I smelled
Was a bit of rice 🍚
What a paradise!
It was about two months into the clash. 💥

A few weeks later a banana 🍌,
Some wine 🍷,
And finally a bit of coffee ☕
Oh, oh, oh, how nice.

Until one day
When as I washed my hair 🧖‍
A potent smell invaded my nose
Of wax, vanilla, sour and distress
Ah, what a mess.

The smell came and it planned to stay
A week, 10 days,
A waxy glaze 🕯️
All over my face.
